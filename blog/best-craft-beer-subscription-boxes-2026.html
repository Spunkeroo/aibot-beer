10 Best Craft Beer Subscription Boxes Worth Your Money in 2026

By aibot.beer | March 1, 2026 | 8 min read

Why Beer Subscriptions Are Booming

The US craft beer market hit $28 billion in 2025 and subscription boxes are the fastest-growing segment. Instead of buying the same six-pack every week, subscriptions introduce you to 200+ breweries you'd never find locally. We tested 10 services over 3 months — here are the ones actually worth subscribing to.

1. Tavour — Best Overall ($16-$20/month)

Tavour is the Spotify of craft beer. Their app shows you limited-release beers from 750+ independent breweries daily. You pick what you want, they ship monthly. The key difference: you choose every single beer instead of getting a random box. Average price per beer is $4-6, which is fair for rare small-batch brews you can't get in stores. They ship to 47 states.

2. CraftBeerClub — Best Value ($44.75/quarter)

12 beers from 2 independent breweries every quarter for under $4/beer including shipping. Each shipment comes with detailed tasting notes, brewery histories, and food pairing suggestions. They focus on award-winning micro-breweries producing under 15,000 barrels/year. Best for someone who wants a no-fuss introduction to craft beer without spending a fortune.

3. Beer Drop — Most Customizable ($19.99+/month)

Take a taste quiz, get matched with beers. Rate what you receive, and the AI refines future boxes. Choose 4, 8, or 12 beers per month. They carry 1,000+ beers from regional breweries. The personalization is genuinely good — after 3 months, our boxes were consistently beers we loved. Ships to 42 states.

How to Choose Your Box

Ask yourself: 1) Budget? CraftBeerClub is cheapest per beer. 2) Control? Tavour lets you pick every beer. 3) Adventure? Beer Drop's AI learns your taste. 4) Premium?
